About Us

Cyber Shield IDN is an Indonesian cloud service provider that focuses on delivering local server infrastructure with low latency, high reliability, and affordable pricing.

Based on our research, international cloud providers such as AWS, Google Cloud, and DigitalOcean offer advanced features but often come with higher costs, complex configuration, and international latency. Cyber Shield IDN provides a simpler and more accessible alternative.

  • Local data center infrastructure
  • Optimized for Indonesian users
  • Easy-to-use cloud services
